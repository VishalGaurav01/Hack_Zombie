About Us

KOSELIG - The Coffee and Art House in the Town of Surat!
For all the art lovers, this is a place that appreciates each art work!

Koselig is based on the idea that why people are much happier in Denmark and Scandinavian countries. We occasionally have Events, at our restaurant
It’s mainly about the Cozy ambience with simple colors (white, brown) and coffee.And also about Norwegian culture which mainly determines, the design style and culture. They manage an Active book club of 70 active members of total 200 members.
With both indoor and rooftop seating areas, and a menu full of drool-worthy sweet treats, Koselig is a perfect cafe for couples in Surat.
